Project Report

1. Introduction & Goal

This project aims to understand micromobility trends in New York City. The Citi Bike system has become a critical part of the city's transportation network. By utilizing data science techniques, we aim to analyze user behavior and traffic flows.

2. Dataset & Methodology

The data used in this project is sourced from the Citi Bike open data portal. The dataset includes trip duration, start/end coordinates, and user types. Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Folium) was used for data cleaning and visualization.

3. Key Insights

  • Weather Impact: Weather conditions influence ride volume by approximately 60%.
  • Commuter Flows: 80% of weekday traffic is directed towards major business districts during rush hours.
  • Tourism: Weekend rides show a distinct pattern centered around parks and waterfronts.
