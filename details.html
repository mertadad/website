Project Report

1. Introduction & Goal

This project aims to understand micromobility trends in New York City.

2. Dataset & Methodology

The data used in this project is sourced from the Citi Bike open data portal.

3. Key Insights

  • Weather Impact: Weather conditions influence ride volume by approximately 60%.
  • Commuter Flows: 80% of weekday traffic is directed towards major business districts.
